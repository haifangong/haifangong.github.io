I am currently a phd candicate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen in CIE, supervised by Prof. Haofeng Li . Before that, I recive the master degree from Computer Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), supervised by Prof. Guanbin Li in 2022. I received the B.
