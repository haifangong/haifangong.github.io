I am currently a PhD candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen in CIE, supervised by Prof. Haofeng Li, Wan Xiang at SRIBD, and Prof. Dong Wang at SSE. Before that, I received my Master's degree in Computer Engineering in 2022 from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), supervised by Prof. Guanbin Li at the HCP Lab. I received my B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology in 2020 from Nanchang University (NCU).

My research interests include domain adaptation, multi-modal learning, medical imaging analysis, and bioinformatics.
