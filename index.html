Biography

I am currently a PostDoctoral Researcher at UPenn, working with Tianyu Han at AIBIL. I received my PhD degree in Computer Information Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen in July 2025, supervised by Haofeng Li, Wan Xiang, and Dong Wang. Previously, I received my Master's degree from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU) in July 2022, supervised by Guanbin Li.

Research Interests LLM for health, domain generalization, multi-modal learning, medical imaging analysis, and bioinformatics.
